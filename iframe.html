Welcome to Jordan

Jordan, a land of ancient wonders and natural beauty, invites you to explore its rich history and breathtaking landscapes. From the majestic ruins of Petra, an ancient city carved into rose-colored cliffs, to the serene waters of the Dead Sea, the lowest point on Earth, Jordan offers a journey through time and culture like no other.

Discovering Jordan's History

Steeped in history, Jordan boasts a legacy dating back to ancient times. From the Nabateans who built the magnificent city of Petra to the Romans who left behind impressive architectural wonders, Jordan's past is a tapestry of civilizations and cultures. Explore the ruins of Jerash, one of the best-preserved Roman cities in the world, or wander through the ancient streets of Amman, where modernity meets tradition.
