Petra - Jewel of Jordan

Carved into the rose-red cliffs of southern Jordan, Petra stands as a testament to the ingenuity and artistry of ancient civilizations. Known as the "Rose City" for the color of its stone, Petra is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

Exploring Petra's History

Founded by the Nabateans over 2,000 years ago, Petra served as a major trading hub and capital city, flourishing with wealth and culture. Its iconic Treasury, carved into the rock face, is just one of many architectural marvels that await exploration within the city.
